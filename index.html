How to Play

Camera

  • Right-drag or Q/E - Rotate the world
  • Middle-drag or WASD - Pan camera
  • Wheel - Zoom in/out

Building

  • Left-click - Place selected block or template
  • Right-click - Remove block
  • 1-0 - Quick-select block types
  • B/X/R/L/F - Switch tools

Heroes

  • Spawn heroes from the Heroes panel (costs 5 food)
  • Click a hero to select, click ground to move
  • Heroes gather nearby resources automatically

Sandbox

  • World tab: regenerate terrain, change seed, time of day
  • Brush size slider for batch terraforming
  • Save/Load buttons store world in browser localStorage
